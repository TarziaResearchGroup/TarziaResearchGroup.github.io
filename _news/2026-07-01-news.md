Andrew published his work on unbiased structure prediction, where they used a series of case studies that push the boundaries
of conventional cage structure prediction approaches and highlight the importance of the new method that is built into 
[cgx](https://cgexplore.readthedocs.io/en/latest/index.html). From molecular building blocks to possible cage structures!
